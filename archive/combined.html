The Problem

You believe in the thesis. But how do you invest in it?

You read the research. You follow the analysts on fintwit and Substack. You’re convinced their portfolio is a winner. But replicating a 100–200 position model portfolio across US, Japanese, Taiwanese, and European exchanges requires $50K–$750K in capital. Many underlying assets don't offer fractional shares. And manually rebalancing weekly is a logistical nightmare. You physically can't do it.

The Solution

Read the research. baskets handles the rest.

Deposit as little as $100 into a managed basket that mirrors your chosen analyst’s exact portfolio weights. When the manager changes their portfolio, Baskets recalculates and pushes updates to IBKR, which auto-rebalances all subscribed accounts. Your capital sits in a segregated account at IBKR, protected by SIPC. You get the same exposure as someone with $500K — at a fraction of the cost and effort.

Thesis-driven investing

Each basket is driven by a deep research thesis — published openly on Substack and Twitter. You can read the manager's reasoning, understand their conviction, and decide if their portfolio aligns with your worldview before putting in a single dollar.

Full transparency

See exactly what's in every basket: each position, its weight, the exchanges it trades on, and a full audit trail of every rebalance. No black boxes, no hidden fees, no "trust us" opacity. You see exactly where your money sits.

Your $100 holds…

A pro-rata slice of every position in the basket. Your $100 is split across 142 positions — NVDA, TSM, ASML, and 139 others — in the exact proportions that a $500K+ portfolio would hold. The tracking error is sub-basis-point.
